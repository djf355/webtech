Becoming an Adult...

4 August, 2016

TL;DR still not an adult.

I graduated from NYU in May, and I've been slumming it with my parents ever since. The grad school process was a bit complicated, as I was determined to go to school overseas, where they fully accept you only after seeing your final transcript. Which meant I needed my exam grades. Which meant the professors needed to grade the exams... which they were doing... very... very... sloooowly.... Anyway, grades went up and then there was this whole debacle with, even though my final transcript was done, some schools wanted my certificate. Which, as with most schools in the US of A, does not come until 3 months after you graduate. Makes total sense. Anyway, through all of that nonsense in the beginning, I just waited around. Finally, things were set with University of Bristol, so that is where I will be headed in September.

Funnily enough, that's where my long-distance boyfriend is currently going to school. No, I did not plan my life around around him. Things actually just worked out really well. I applied to the University of Bristol just to see if I could get in, but set my sights elsewhere. In the end, it was the best university that I was accepted to, so that made my decision. So, here I go.
